# CIS Controls v8

**Canonical source:** Center for Internet Security, CIS Controls v8
(`spec:cis-controls-v8`).
**Role:** vulnerability management and detection & response safeguards.

## What it defines
18 prioritized controls with concrete safeguards, ordered by defensive value:
inventory of assets and software, data protection, secure configuration,
account and access management, continuous vulnerability management, audit log
management, malware defenses, and incident response. Implementation Groups
(IG1–IG3) scale the safeguards to organization maturity.

## Role in the crosswalk
CIS gives the operational safeguards a governed AI system runs day to day:
scanning dependencies and images, patch SLAs, log management, and account
hygiene. It keeps "robustness & accuracy" and "risk management" concrete and
prioritized.

## Caveat
CIS is a prioritized safeguard set, not a certification or a legal standard.
Prioritization guides where to start; it does not excuse the safeguards left for
later, which must be tracked as accepted risk.
